# Project Summary
AmanahLife is a subscription-based SaaS platform designed for Muslim individuals and families, enhancing productivity and lifestyle management through AI-driven insights. It provides tools for daily planning, habit tracking, and personal organization, addressing spiritual, financial, and wellness needs. Key features include a Family Budget Planner, personalized AI insights, and lifestyle tracking tools, all powered by a robust Node.js and Supabase architecture. AmanahLife aims to improve user productivity and well-being while aligning with their values and beliefs.

# Project Module Description
The project consists of various functional modules:
- **Dashboard:** Summarizes tasks, prayers, goals, and balances.
- **Tasks:** Manages tasks with recurrence, priorities, and sharing.
- **Goals:** Tracks personal, financial, spiritual, and family goals.
- **Finance:** Manages income/expenses, budgets, and Zakat & Giving Tracker.
- **Prayer:** Provides prayer times and tracking.
- **Spiritual:** Features Quran tracking, Dhikr counter, and Ramadan tools.
- **Wellness:** Monitors mood, sleep, hydration, and stress.
- **AI Insights:** Offers personalized tips based on user data.
- **Search Features:** Classic and Smart Search for content and data.
- **Planner:** Task management with agenda views.
- **Notifications:** Custom alerts for tasks and prayers.
- **Subscriptions:** Manages user subscriptions with multi-currency support.
- **Data Export:** Allows exporting financial and goal data.
- **RTL Support:** Supports right-to-left language layouts.
- **AI Planning Support:** AI-powered scheduling for premium users.
- **Promo Banner:** Offers discounts for free users.
- **Family Budget Planner:** Multi-currency income/expense tracking.
- **Financial Dashboard:** Displays KPIs and savings goals.
- **Halal Investment Tools:** Islamic screening criteria and calculators.
- **Ramadan & Eid Planner:** Meal planning and charity tracking.
- **Streaks & Gamification:** XP system and badges for achievements.
- **Smart Briefings:** Daily briefing widget.
- **Giving Tracker:** Localized support for currency calculations.
- **Progress Analytics:** Visualizes individual and family progress.
- **Blog:** Section for SEO-optimized articles.
- **Family Shared Dashboard:** Dashboard for family members.
- **Bill Reminders:** Manages recurring bills with notifications.
- **Dua of the Day:** Daily dua display and notifications.
- **AI Life Coach:** Personalized coaching with chat UI.
- **Weekly Life Score:** Scoring across life dimensions.
- **Smart Savings Challenges:** Gamified savings tracking.
- **Receipt Scanner:** For scanning receipts and expense tracking.
- **Email Digest Toggle:** Manage weekly email subscriptions.
- **Islamic Calendar:** Highlights current Hijri month and events.
- **Search History:** Saves recent searches for quick access.
- **Legal Pages:** Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and more.

# Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** React 18, Vite, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui, Framer Motion
- **Backend:** Node.js, Supabase
- **Database:** PostgreSQL (Supabase)
- **Payment Processing:** Lemon Squeezy (sole provider)
