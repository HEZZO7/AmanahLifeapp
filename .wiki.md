# Project Summary
AmanahLife is a comprehensive Islamic lifestyle management SaaS platform designed to empower Muslim individuals and families in managing their spiritual, financial, and wellness needs. Key features include prayer tracking, financial management, and wellness monitoring, enriching daily lives and spiritual practices. Built on a Node.js + Supabase architecture, the platform ensures scalability and maintainability for rapid feature development and an enhanced user experience. Recent enhancements include a Family Budget Planner, Financial Dashboard, Halal Investment tools, and Ramadan & Eid Planner, along with AI-powered features and a new promotional banner encouraging free-tier users to subscribe with a discount on their first month.

# Project Module Description
The project consists of several functional modules:
- **Dashboard:** Displays daily summaries including tasks, prayers, goals, and balance.
- **Tasks:** Manages tasks with recurrence, priorities, and family sharing.
- **Goals:** Tracks personal, financial, spiritual, and family goals with progress monitoring.
- **Finance:** Handles income/expenses, budgets, and includes a Zakat calculator.
- **Prayer:** Provides prayer times, tracking, and consistency charts.
- **Spiritual:** Features Quran tracking, Dhikr counter, Duas collection, and Ramadan tools.
- **Wellness:** Monitors mood, sleep, hydration, and stress levels with daily logging.
- **AI Insights:** Offers personalized tips and monthly life reviews based on user data.
- **Planner:** Provides agenda, weekly, and monthly views for task management.
- **Settings:** Manages user preferences including theme, language, and notifications.
- **Notifications:** Customizable alerts for tasks, prayers, and goals.
- **Subscriptions:** Manages user subscriptions through Stripe, including new premium features and a Customer Portal for managing payment methods and viewing invoices.
- **Data Export:** Allows exporting financial and goal data as CSV files.
- **RTL Support:** Supports right-to-left language layouts.
- **AI Planning Support:** Offers AI-powered scheduling and planning features for premium users.
- **Smart Search:** Provides natural language search across app data for premium users.
- **Promo Banner:** A promotional banner for free users, offering a discount on their first month after one week of usage.
- **Family Budget Planner:** Includes family profiles, annual goals, and multi-currency income/expense tracking.
- **Financial Dashboard:** Displays KPIs, expense breakdowns, and savings goals (premium feature).
- **Halal Investment Tools:** Islamic screening criteria, portfolio tracker, and calculators for home ownership.
- **Ramadan & Eid Planner:** 30-day fasting grid, meal planning, and charity tracker.
- **Streaks & Gamification:** XP system and badges for achievements in prayers, Quran, and savings.
- **Smart Briefings:** Daily briefing widget on the homepage.

- **Backend:** Node.js, Supabase
- **Database:** PostgreSQL (Supabase)
- **Payment Processing:** Stripe
