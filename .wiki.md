# Project Summary
AmanahLife is an Islamic lifestyle management SaaS platform designed to empower Muslim individuals and families to manage their spiritual, financial, and wellness needs. Utilizing AI-driven insights, it offers tools for prayer tracking, financial management, and wellness monitoring. Built on a scalable Node.js + Supabase architecture, the platform supports rapid feature development. Recent enhancements include multi-payment support with Stripe, Lemon Squeezy, and Paddle, as well as a Family Budget Planner, Financial Dashboard, Halal Investment tools, Ramadan & Eid Planner, and improved currency handling for Zakat calculations.

# Project Module Description
The project consists of several functional modules:
- **Dashboard:** Daily summaries including tasks, prayers, goals, and balance.
- **Tasks:** Manages tasks with recurrence, priorities, and family sharing.
- **Goals:** Tracks personal, financial, spiritual, and family goals with progress monitoring.
- **Finance:** Handles income/expenses, budgets, and includes a Zakat calculator with multi-currency support.
- **Prayer:** Provides prayer times, tracking, and consistency charts.
- **Spiritual:** Features Quran tracking, Dhikr counter, Duas collection, and Ramadan tools.
- **Wellness:** Monitors mood, sleep, hydration, and stress levels with daily logging.
- **AI Insights:** Offers personalized tips and monthly life reviews based on user data.
- **Classic Search:** Text-based search feature across app content.
- **Smart Search:** Natural language search across app data for premium users.
- **Planner:** Agenda, weekly, and monthly views for task management.
- **Notifications:** Customizable alerts for tasks, prayers, and goals.
- **Subscriptions:** Manages user subscriptions through multiple payment providers, including Stripe, Lemon Squeezy, and Paddle.
- **Data Export:** Allows exporting financial and goal data as CSV files.
- **RTL Support:** Supports right-to-left language layouts.
- **AI Planning Support:** AI-powered scheduling and planning for premium users.
- **Promo Banner:** Promotional banner for free users offering discounts.
- **Family Budget Planner:** Family profiles, annual goals, and multi-currency income/expense tracking.
- **Financial Dashboard:** Displays KPIs, expense breakdowns, and savings goals (premium feature).
- **Halal Investment Tools:** Islamic screening criteria, portfolio tracker, and calculators for home ownership.
- **Ramadan & Eid Planner:** 30-day fasting grid, meal planning, and charity tracker.
- **Streaks & Gamification:** XP system and badges for achievements in prayers, Quran, and savings.
- **Smart Briefings:** Daily briefing widget on the homepage.
- **Zakat Calculator:** Localized with Arabic support, providing comprehensive currency options and calculations.
- **Progress Analytics:** New module for visualizing individual and family progress through charts and statistics.

# Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** React 18, Vite, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui, Framer Motion
- **Backend:** Node.js, Supabase
- **Database:** PostgreSQL (Supabase)
- **Payment Processing:** Stripe, Lemon Squeezy, Paddle
