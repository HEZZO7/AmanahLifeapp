# Project Summary
AmanahLife is a subscription-based productivity and lifestyle management SaaS platform tailored for Muslim individuals and families. It provides integrated tools for daily planning, habit tracking, and personal organization, addressing spiritual, financial, and wellness needs through AI-driven insights. Key features include a Family Budget Planner, lifestyle tracking, wellness monitoring, and personalized AI insights, all built on a scalable Node.js + Supabase architecture. The platform enhances user experience with live exchange rates for financial transactions.

# Project Module Description
The project consists of several functional modules:
- **Dashboard:** Daily summaries including tasks, prayers, goals, and balance.
- **Tasks:** Manages tasks with recurrence, priorities, and family sharing.
- **Goals:** Tracks personal, financial, spiritual, and family goals with progress monitoring.
- **Finance:** Handles income/expenses, budgets, and includes a Giving Tracker with multi-currency support.
- **Prayer:** Provides prayer times, tracking, and consistency charts.
- **Spiritual:** Features Quran tracking, Dhikr counter, Duas collection, and Ramadan tools.
- **Wellness:** Monitors mood, sleep, hydration, and stress levels with daily logging.
- **AI Insights:** Offers personalized tips and monthly life reviews based on user data.
- **Classic Search:** Text-based search feature across app content.
- **Smart Search:** Natural language search across app data for premium users.
- **Planner:** Agenda, weekly, and monthly views for task management.
- **Notifications:** Customizable alerts for tasks, prayers, and goals.
- **Subscriptions:** Manages user subscriptions through Lemon Squeezy, now with multi-currency pricing support and an inline currency selector.
- **Data Export:** Allows exporting financial and goal data as CSV files.
- **RTL Support:** Supports right-to-left language layouts.
- **AI Planning Support:** AI-powered scheduling and planning for premium users.
- **Promo Banner:** Promotional banner for free users offering discounts.
- **Family Budget Planner:** Family profiles, annual goals, and multi-currency income/expense tracking.
- **Financial Dashboard:** Displays KPIs, expense breakdowns, and savings goals (premium feature).
- **Halal Investment Tools:** Islamic screening criteria, portfolio tracker, and calculators for home ownership.
- **Ramadan & Eid Planner:** 30-day fasting grid, meal planning, and charity tracker.
- **Streaks & Gamification:** XP system and badges for achievements in prayers, Quran, and savings.
- **Smart Briefings:** Daily briefing widget on the homepage.
- **Giving Tracker:** Localized with Arabic support, providing comprehensive currency options and calculations.
- **Progress Analytics:** Visualizes individual and family progress through charts and statistics.
- **Blog:** A section for SEO-optimized articles to enhance user engagement and provide valuable content.
- **Family Shared Dashboard:** A page for family members to view shared budget, goals, and prayer streaks together.
- **Bill Reminders & Auto-tracking:** Manages recurring bills with notifications for due dates.
- **Dua of the Day + Daily Notifications:** Displays a daily rotating dua and enables daily notifications.
- **AI Life Coach:** Personalized coaching with chat UI, goal-based advice, habit suggestions, and motivational insights.
- **Weekly Life Score:** Scoring across 5 life dimensions with visual trends and personalized recommendations.
- **Smart Savings Challenges:** Gamified savings challenges with progress tracking, milestone celebrations, and daily reminder notifications.
- **Receipt Scanner:** Upload/camera UI for scanning receipts, categorization, and expense tracking.

# Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** React 18, Vite, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui, Framer Motion
- **Backend:** Node.js, Supabase
- **Database:** PostgreSQL (Supabase)
- **Payment Processing:** Lemon Squeezy (sole provider)
